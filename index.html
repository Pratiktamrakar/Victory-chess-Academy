Our Story

Developing Chess Champions in New Raipur

Victory Chess Academy was founded with one goal: provide high-quality chess coaching to students in New Raipur. We believe chess builds critical thinking, patience, and strategic vision.

Our focus is on transforming beginners and intermediate players (under 1300 rating) into confident, competitive chess players through structured curriculum and personal attention.
